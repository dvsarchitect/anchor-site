About The Anchor

The Anchor is for men (and anyone) who feel adrift—caught between what’s true and what’s loud. We return to Scripture, slowly and honestly, because the goal isn’t to win arguments; it’s to find Jesus—and follow Him.

On Mondays you’ll get a short, practical word. On Fridays, a deeper study that meets you where you are. No posturing. No hot takes. Just the Word, applied.
